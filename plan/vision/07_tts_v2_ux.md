# UX: TTS v2 Features

**PRD:** [07_tts_v2_prd.md](07_tts_v2_prd.md)

## Sleep Timer

Tap sleep icon → Options:
- 15 min / 30 min / 45 min / 1 hour
- End of chapter
- Custom time

Display: Timer badge on player, countdown in status

## Smart Speed

Settings → Audio → Smart Speed
- Off / Light / Medium / Aggressive
- Preview: "Saves ~15% time"

## Car Mode

Entry: Bluetooth auto-detect or manual
- 3 giant buttons
- High contrast
- Voice: "Hey Versicle, skip chapter"

## Chapter Pre-roll

"Chapter 14: The Sleeper Must Awaken"
