Fix predictable loading behavior of loadSection in AudioPlayerService

This change ensures that `loadSection` behaves consistently by validating
the context (bookId) has not changed after resolving the playlistPromise.
This prevents enqueued background load requests from firing on the wrong book
when rapidly switching active books, analogous to what was already done for
`loadSectionBySectionId`.

It also adds an explicit test for `loadSection` race conditions mimicking
the fix previously provided for `loadSectionBySectionId`.
